Our Story

We started New England Smokery because we wanted to share the rich, complex flavors that only slow smoking can achieve. We were frustrated with the lack of quality smoked goods available, so we decided to create our own.

Our Philosophy

We're committed to using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques. We meticulously select premium cheeses and nuts, locally sourced vegetables and smoke them low and slow over hickory, applewood, and mesquite. This patient process allows the smoke to penetrate deeply, infusing each ingredient with a depth of flavor that's simply unmatched.

Our Commitment to Quality

We believe that quality is paramount. That's why we hand-select each ingredient, monitor our smoking process meticulously, and use sustainable practices. We're dedicated to bringing you smoked goods that are not only delicious but also crafted with integrity.

Our Promise
